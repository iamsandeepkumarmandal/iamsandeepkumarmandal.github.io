Technolexis

Second floor, Sri Guruprasadam, Shyamlal Buildings, Begumpet
+91-9347517427

Established in May 2014, Technolexis is a fast moving and leading rapid prototyping, tooling and low-volume manufacturing company. We have been driven by the needs of our customers and give innovators the powerful tool of digital manufacturing. We lead them to unleash their creativity into their respective industries and explore possibilities in new frontiers thereby making an indelible mark in the 3D Printing and IOT Embedded industry.

We have unrivalled specialist knowledge of the entire product range who focus on sharing this knowledge with engineering students by setting R&D centres in academic institutions and giving technical training.

Technolexis is changing the way product development and manufacturing professionals source small quantity production and custom-designed parts in industries such as automotive, aerospace, healthcare, art and design and, consumer goods potentially changing the industry for the better. We have forged strong relationships with the leaders in 3D printing, metrology and 3D scanning systems to deliver efficient systems to businesses across industry segments.
