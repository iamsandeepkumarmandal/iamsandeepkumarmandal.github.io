our team

Kevin Boake

Kevin Boake,

Founder and EVP Sales (UK)

Kevin has worked in the supply chain planning field for over 25 years and lead a major global supply chain software business for 10 of these years. Through his dealings with over 200 companies globally, he has gained extensive knowledge of supply chain planning from a software, consultancy and business process outsourcing point of view. Kevin is a DDP Instructor and currently lectures on forecasting, inventory, capacity and production planning at both Warwick University in the UK and Polytech University in Hong Kong.

Laurent Vigouroux

Laurent Vigouroux,

CEO B2Wise France (Franchise)

Laurent has worked for 16 year in the automotive and industrial industries applying Lean and best practices in supply chain management. In 2013 he implemented the first DDMRP project in France which went on to win the king of Supply chain award in 2015. Laurent is now 100% dedicated to DDMRP and has worked on over 25 projects. Laurent also developed the DDBRIX management game which has now been played by over 2000 people.

Trevor Boake

Trevor Boake,

Head of Development (USA)

Trevor has spent over 22 years developing and implementing software applications. His career started as a supply chain consultant before spending 7 years developing and implementing business intelligence solutions for many of the Fortune 500 companies. Before joining B2Wise, Trevor was the IT Director of a NYSE listed, financial services firm and was responsible for the global development team.

Daniel Eliades

Daniel Eliades,

Head of UI (USA)

Daniel is a full-stack developer with over 12 years’ experience developing custom web-based applications.  He received his M.S. degree in Computer Science from Stevens Institute of Technology. Prior to joining B2Wise Daniel was a VP in Application Development for a NYSE listed firm where he developed multiple internal and external web-based applications. Daniel has extensive skills in data mining, business intelligence and mobile application development
