about us
(who we are)

Anviti Insurance Brokers Private Limited is in the process of setting up its Insurance Broking operations in India. We intend to be a composite insurance broker delivering insurance and reinsurance broking services to our valued clients in India, driven by excellence, innovation and integrity.

Our core focus will be on the Non-life segment (H&B and P&C specialties). We at Anviti aspire to attain a market leadership position in the B2B insurance broking market by delivering differentiated, technology led insurance broking services.

Our Promoters

Anviti is promoted by Catamaran, the proprietary investment firm of Mr N.R. Narayana Murthy, founder of Infosys Limited. Catamaran is a leading Indian entrepreneurial, technology and evergreen investment firm that creates, partners with and funds innovative businesses globally. This Insurance broking investment is a part of Catamaran’s continuing mission to support businesses that are changing the economic fabric of India.

Catamaran invests in opportunities that are fundamentally altering the structure of their industries in India, or that are seeking to gain global reach and repute. Catamaran works with both private and public companies, run by either home-grown entrepreneurs or global leaders. Catamaran also builds such businesses in-house as green-field ventures.
