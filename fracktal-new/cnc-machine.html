CNC Overview

Tooling

CNC machining, due to its precision and accuracy, is perfect choice for creating master patterns for moulds.

Low-volume Production

Given the relatively short lead times involved and the incredible variety of materials that can be machined, CNC machining is a good option for rapid prototyping.

Reliable End-Use Production

CNC offers unparalleled precision and repeatability when it comes to end-use production. It is capable of tight tolerances and is usable with a variety of materials.
