Summary

When videos are the first thing that grab attention in your website, then why not capture the lead details while watching the video. Be it your webinar video or product overview, ensuring you grab your lead’s information at the right time and right place is important. It was found that viewers who clicked on CTA at the end of videos converted to trials at a rate of 21% vs 1% of viewers who didn’t get the CTA.


In this webinar, we’ll see how you can generate leads faster with video marketing. With live demo we’ll show how you can directly tie videos to your marketing ROI.
